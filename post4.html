Easy life hacks for day to day life

Life can get hectic, but small changes in your daily routine can make a big difference. Here are some easy life hacks to simplify your day-to-day activities and make life a bit easier:

1. Use a Binder Clip as a Cable Organizer

Attach a binder clip to the edge of your desk and thread your cables through the metal arms. This keeps your cables organized and prevents them from tangling or falling behind your desk.

2. Freshen Up Your Shoes with Baking Soda

Sprinkle baking soda inside your shoes and leave it overnight. Shake out the baking soda before wearing them. This simple trick absorbs moisture and odors, keeping your shoes smelling fresh.

3. Use a Rubber Band to Open Stubborn Jars

Wrap a rubber band around the lid of a stubborn jar to get a better grip. The added friction makes it easier to twist off the lid and open the jar without straining your hands.

4. Reinforce Your Phone Charging Cable

To prevent your phone's charging cable from fraying, use a spring from an old pen and wrap it around the base of the cable. This simple reinforcement helps prevent bending and breaking.

5. Organize Your Fridge with Lazy Susans

Place Lazy Susans in your fridge to make it easier to access items stored in the back. This helps you keep everything organized and prevents food from getting lost or forgotten.
